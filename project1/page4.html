Jiazhen Luo was born in Nanjing, Jiangsu Province, China, and like many Chinese children, did not receive any art education as a child, could not paint, did not understand music, and dreamed of becoming a water truck driver during his childhood. In 2020, In 2020, with a film camera from his high school teacher, he began to dabble in photography and art. In 2023, he decided to study abroad in the United States. After experiencing overwhelming self-struggle and a series of setbacks, he managed to get into a decent art school with luck and her parents' hard work, beginning his formal study of art. Following his parents' guidance and advice, he pragmatically gave up photography to study programming and new media there. Due to his shyness and overthinking, he tended to avoid social media and rarely published his works publicly. Relying on the few years of English he learned in high school for exams, he never accurately expressed his works and ideas to his teachers. During his university years, he was confused and unsure about what he really wanted to study, focusing solely on completing assignments for various subjects and learning English.
